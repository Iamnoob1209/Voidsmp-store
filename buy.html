HOW TO BUY A RANK

Quick & easy process through Discord

Purchase Steps

1

Join Our Discord Server

Click the button below to join the VoidSMP Discord server. If you're already a member, you can skip this step.

2

Create a Ticket

Go to the #create-ticket channel and click on the ticket button to open a new support ticket. Select "Rank Purchase" as the ticket type.

3

Choose Your Rank

In your ticket, let our staff know which rank you want to purchase:

  • Wraith - ₹90
  • Phantom - ₹110
  • Specter - ₹150
  • Void King - ₹200
4

Complete Payment

Our staff will provide you with payment details. Complete the payment through the provided method (UPI, PayPal, etc.).

5

Send Payment Proof

After payment, send a screenshot of your payment confirmation in the ticket. Include your Minecraft username so we can apply the rank to the correct account.

6

Receive Your Rank

Once payment is verified, your rank will be applied within a few minutes. You'll receive a confirmation message and can start enjoying your new perks!

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive my rank?

Once payment is verified, ranks are usually applied within 5-30 minutes depending on staff availability. During peak times, it may take up to a few hours.

Is my rank permanent?

Yes! All ranks are permanent and will never expire. You keep your perks forever.

Can I upgrade my rank later?

Absolutely! If you want to upgrade to a higher rank, just create another ticket. You'll only need to pay the difference between your current rank and the new one.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, PayPal, and other payment methods. Ask in your ticket for available options.

I have more questions. How can I contact support?

Create a ticket in our Discord server and our staff will be happy to help with any questions!
